Mission

ElVetrina is in a mission to bring to the market a technological platform that will enable businesses to transform and to provide a mean for enhancing the customer engagement and experience along with the tools to measure the effectiveness and that will monetize on location-based assets.

ElVetrina core belief is that businesses succeed when solving real life problems. ElVetrina mission is to bring technology not for the sake of technology but when it is aiming to address the issues and concerns of a real life situation.



ElVetrina

ElVetrina, Inc. is a technology start-up providing location-aware and proximity marketing analytics and engagement platform. We provide a platform that can not only enhance how businesses engage and attract customers but also e enable businesses to transform to capitalize on their location-based assets and to monetize those.
